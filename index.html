    • Minimal design?

    Modern websites tend to emphasize sleek design at the expense of simplicity (and sometimes function). Infinite scrolling, animated dropdown menus, obtuse navigation, and long loading times for a website designed to convey only a few lines of text are clear examples of modern design losing sight of what is important. In the interest of returning to basics, I've used a minimalist approach to delivering content.
